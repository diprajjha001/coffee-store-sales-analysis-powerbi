# ☕ Coffee Store Sales & Revenue Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

An interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes coffee store sales, revenue trends, and customer insights to drive data-driven business decisions.

---

## 📊 Project Overview

This project showcases a dynamic Power BI dashboard built to analyze coffee store performance across multiple dimensions such as revenue, transactions, profit, store types, and product categories.

The dashboard transforms raw data into meaningful insights, helping stakeholders understand business performance and identify growth opportunities.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and revenue performance  
- Track monthly revenue trends and growth patterns  
- Compare performance across store types (Urban vs Suburban)  
- Identify top-performing product categories  
- Discover opportunities for business expansion  

---

## 📌 Key Insights

- Urban stores contribute the majority of total revenue  
- Suburban stores have lower contribution, indicating growth potential  
- Coffee is the top-performing category  
- Revenue trends show fluctuations across months  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

- Interactive filters (Year, Region, Segment)  
- Revenue trend and growth comparison  
- Store-wise performance analysis  
- Category-wise revenue breakdown  
- Clean and intuitive visual design  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Power BI  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- Data Modeling
